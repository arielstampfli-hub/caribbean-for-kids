Our story

Made by Caribbean people, for Caribbean kids

Caribbean for Kids began with a simple idea: children deserve learning materials that look like home. What started as one colouring book has grown into a series that celebrates the flags, wildlife, flavours and wonder of the islands — all designed to make kids excited to learn.

Every book is crafted to be fun first and educational always, so curiosity grows page after page.
